CertainTeed Select ShingleMaster vs. Owens Corning Preferred: What Manufacturer Certifications Actually Mean

May 4, 2026 · 7 min read · JK Prestige Constructor Corp

---

When you hire a roofing contractor in Jacksonville FL, you will encounter contractors who list manufacturer certifications as selling points. What those certifications actually mean — what they require of the contractor, what warranties they unlock for you, and why they matter — is rarely explained. Here is the plain-English breakdown of the two programs most relevant to residential asphalt shingle work, plus context on GAF Master Elite, which JK Prestige Constructor also holds.

What Manufacturer Certifications Require of Roofing Contractors

Manufacturer certification programs are not free to join and not automatic. Each program has its own requirements, but the common elements across CertainTeed, Owens Corning, and GAF include:

  • Demonstrated installation training. Installers must complete manufacturer-approved training, not generic trade training. CertainTeed's Shingle Applicator Training, Owens Corning's program at their training centers, and GAF's CARE (Continuing Application and Roofing Education) course each test installation knowledge specific to that manufacturer's product systems.
  • Active license and insurance verification. Manufacturers verify contractor licensing and insurance before granting certification and during annual renewal. In Florida, this means an active state-issued roofing license, current general liability, and workers' compensation — not just a business registration.
  • Warranty claims track record. Manufacturers audit their certified contractors' warranty claim history. A certified contractor with a pattern of installation-related warranty claims loses certification. This creates accountability that a non-certified contractor does not face.
  • Business standing. BBB standing, complaint resolution history, and financial stability are reviewed. Programs vary in rigor, but each sets a minimum threshold.

A contractor who cannot get or keep manufacturer certification has typically failed one of these checks. When you hire a non-certified contractor, you have no third-party verification that they meet any of these standards.

CertainTeed Select ShingleMaster: What the Tier Provides

CertainTeed's contractor program has two tiers: ShingleMaster and Select ShingleMaster. JK Prestige holds the Select ShingleMaster designation, which is the upper tier.

To earn Select ShingleMaster status, CertainTeed requires:

  • Completion of the Shingle Applicator Training (SAT) course by installers
  • A minimum percentage of CertainTeed product use in installations
  • Demonstration of business and insurance credentials
  • Annual renewal with continuing training

What Select ShingleMaster unlocks for the homeowner:

The primary consumer benefit is access to SureStart Plus warranty coverage. This is CertainTeed's highest residential warranty tier. Standard CertainTeed warranties offered by non-certified contractors cover manufacturing defects but do not cover labor or accessory materials. SureStart Plus, available exclusively through Select ShingleMaster contractors, adds:

  • Coverage for installation labor costs during the initial coverage period (typically 10 years, but varies by product)
  • Coverage for all CertainTeed accessory products (underlayment, starter strips, hip and ridge shingles) installed as part of the system
  • Transferability to a subsequent owner, which adds value if the home is sold

Landmark Pro, CertainTeed's popular mid-grade residential shingle, qualifies for SureStart Plus when installed by a Select ShingleMaster contractor. Landmark Premium and Carriage House products also qualify. The difference in warranty coverage between a Select ShingleMaster install and a non-certified install on the same product can be $3,000 to $8,000 in potential labor cost coverage over the initial coverage period.

Owens Corning Preferred: What the Tier Provides

Owens Corning runs a three-tier program: Preferred, Preferred Platinum, and Platinum Preferred (their top tier). JK Prestige Constructor holds Preferred certification.

Owens Corning's Preferred certification requires:

  • Completion of Owens Corning installation training
  • Current licensing and insurance verification
  • Business standing review

What Preferred certification unlocks for the homeowner:

Owens Corning's Preferred Protection warranty is the enhanced warranty tier available through Preferred and higher contractors. Compared to standard limited warranties, Preferred Protection adds:

  • Extended labor coverage on qualifying products during the initial warranty period
  • Reduced-cost upgrade to Platinum Protection if the homeowner installs a qualifying Owens Corning Total Protection Roofing System (shingles, underlayment, ventilation, starter, and ridge products all from OC)
  • Transferability to subsequent owners (prorated after year 5)

Owens Corning's Duration shingle series — Duration, Duration Premium, Duration Flex — are among the most installed residential products in the Jacksonville market. A Preferred contractor installing Duration shingles with the full Total Protection system triggers the longest available warranty terms.

GAF Master Elite: Where It Fits

GAF is the largest roofing manufacturer in North America by production volume. Their top contractor tier, Master Elite, is held by approximately 2% of roofing contractors nationally. JK Prestige Constructor holds Master Elite designation.

GAF's Master Elite requirements are the most demanding of the three programs:

  • Only 2% of all roofing contractors in the country qualify at any given time
  • Requires documented license, full insurance, factory training, and a strong warranty claims record
  • Annual renewal with performance review

What Master Elite unlocks for the homeowner:

The key consumer benefit of a GAF Master Elite installation is access to the Golden Pledge Limited Warranty — GAF's highest-tier offering. Standard GAF warranties available through any contractor cover manufacturing defects only. The Golden Pledge adds:

  • 25-year workmanship coverage — this is labor, not just materials
  • Coverage for all System Plus components if a qualifying GAF roofing system is installed (Timberline shingles, GAF underlayment, starter, ventilation, ridge cap)
  • Backed by GAF's warranty team — if a Master Elite contractor disputes a workmanship claim and cannot resolve it, GAF steps in as the underwriter

No other tier in GAF's program offers 25-year workmanship coverage. This is materially different from the standard 1-year contractor labor warranty that most non-certified roofers offer.

Why Certification Creates Accountability Non-Certified Contractors Lack

Here is the mechanism that matters: a certified contractor has an economic relationship with the manufacturer that a non-certified contractor does not. If JK Prestige has an installation-related warranty claim on a GAF job, GAF investigates it. If the claim reflects a legitimate installation error on our part, GAF's warranty team resolves it — and our Master Elite standing reflects our claims history.

A non-certified contractor has no such relationship. If their work fails and they dispute the claim or go out of business, you are left with a manufacturer's materials warranty that does not cover labor costs, filing a claim directly with the manufacturer for a defect the manufacturer will need to determine independently.

This is not a hypothetical risk in Florida. The Jacksonville roofing market sees contractor turnover after every major storm event — crews that mobilize for storm work, collect payment, and are unreachable 18 months later when water damage appears at a flashing detail. Manufacturer certification does not guarantee a contractor stays in business, but it does provide a secondary layer of warranty backing through the manufacturer.

JK Prestige Constructor holds GAF Master Elite, CertainTeed Select ShingleMaster, and Owens Corning Preferred. Holding all three means:

  • We are not locked into one manufacturer's product line. We specify the system that best fits the roof geometry, performance requirements, and homeowner budget — not the system that hits a sales minimum.
  • Homeowners get access to each manufacturer's highest warranty tiers on the product we install.
  • Our installation training and claims record is verified by three independent programs simultaneously.

For a Jacksonville homeowner comparing certified contractors: ask each contractor which specific tier they hold, not just whether they are "certified." ShingleMaster and Select ShingleMaster are different programs with different warranty outcomes. Preferred and Platinum Preferred are different Owens Corning tiers.
