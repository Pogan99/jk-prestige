Roofing Services in St. Augustine, FL

St. Augustine and St. Johns County present a uniquely diverse roofing landscape: historic homes in the downtown area with specialized preservation requirements, mid-century suburban homes in West Augustine, rapidly growing new construction in World Golf Village and Palencia, and coastal properties along Vilano Beach and St. Augustine Beach. JK Prestige Constructor Corp serves all of St. Johns County from Jacksonville, approximately 35–50 minutes from most St. Augustine addresses. We are licensed in St. Johns County, pull all required permits from the St. Johns County Building Department, and have experience with the county's inspection process and timelines.

St. Augustine Roofing FAQ

How far is JK Prestige from St. Augustine?

Approximately 35–50 minutes from most St. Augustine addresses. We serve St. Johns County regularly and have experience with county permit requirements and inspection timelines.

Does my historic St. Augustine home need special roofing approval?

Homes within the St. Augustine Historic District may require approval from the Historic Architectural Review Board (HARB) for changes in roofing material or color that affect historic character. We can advise on what typically triggers review and provide documentation for your submission.

What roofing systems are popular in Palencia and World Golf Village?

Tile roofing is predominant in both communities, reflecting St. Johns County's preference for Mediterranean and Spanish Colonial architectural styles. We handle both concrete and clay tile, underlayment replacement, and structural assessment for tile loading.
