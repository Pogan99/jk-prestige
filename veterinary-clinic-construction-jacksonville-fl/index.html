Veterinary Clinic Construction in Jacksonville: Specialty Requirements and Process

Veterinary clinic construction combines medical-grade construction requirements with the unique needs of animal care — heavy-duty drainage, sound-dampening between kennel areas, specialized HVAC for odor control, and durable surfaces that withstand constant cleaning.

JK Prestige works with veterinarians and practice management consultants to develop floor plans and construction sequences that serve the specific workflow of your practice. We've built exam rooms, surgery suites, diagnostic areas, and boarding kennels across Northeast Florida.

Florida requires all veterinary clinic construction to be performed by licensed general contractors. Permits are required for electrical, plumbing, mechanical, and any structural work.
