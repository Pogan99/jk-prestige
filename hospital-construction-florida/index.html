Medical Facility Builder

ICRA-compliant healthcare construction in occupied and greenfield environments across Florida.

JK Prestige Constructor Corp delivers licensed, ICRA-compliant hospital and medical facility construction across Florida, with operational headquarters in Jacksonville, FL 32202. Healthcare construction demands a level of procedural discipline that exceeds standard commercial building — active infection control protocols, NFPA 99 medical gas compliance, radiation shielding coordination for imaging suites, and continuous coordination with facility management to preserve patient safety during construction activity. Our teams are trained in ICRA Class III and IV procedures, maintain negative pressure containment in occupied zones, and follow documented daily inspection checklists required by Joint Commission environments of care standards. We have delivered outpatient towers, ambulatory surgery centers, imaging suites, medical office buildings, and hospital renovation packages ranging from 8,000 to over 320,000 square feet across Florida. Our preconstruction team builds phasing plans that respect active clinical operations — off-hours work windows, emergency egress requirements, noise and vibration mitigation — coordinated directly with the facility's infection control nurse and facilities director. Whether you are a hospital system planning a new patient tower, a health system expanding outpatient capacity, or a physician group building a specialty clinic, JK Prestige Constructor brings the technical discipline and operational reliability your project demands.
