About This Service

JK Prestige Constructor provides licensed roofing services throughout Clay County — including Fleming Island, Orange Park, Middleburg, Green Cove Springs, and Oakleaf. We install asphalt shingles, metal roofing, and flat roofing systems for both residential and commercial properties.

Clay County's mix of established neighborhoods and new growth communities gives us experience with everything from aging roofs in mature subdivisions to new construction roofing on active builds. All work is permitted and inspected through Clay County Building Services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you serve Fleming Island for roofing?

Yes. Fleming Island is one of our primary Clay County service areas. We handle roof replacements, repairs, and inspections throughout Fleming Island.

What roofing materials are best in Clay County FL?

Architectural asphalt shingles are the most common choice in Clay County. Metal roofing is growing in popularity for its longevity and wind resistance.

Do you help with insurance claims in Clay County?

How much does a roof replacement cost in Clay County?

How long does roofing take in Clay County?

Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1 to 2 days. Larger or more complex roofs may take 3 to 4 days.
