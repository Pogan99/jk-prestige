Our VE Process

A disciplined four-phase approach to preconstruction cost optimization.

01

Document Review & Baseline

Complete review of construction documents to establish baseline cost model. Identify cost drivers, specification premiums, and preliminary VE opportunity areas.

02

VE Workshop

Structured session with owner and design team. Present VE items with cost-benefit analysis, performance equivalence documentation, and owner approval/rejection decision tree.

03

BIM Clash Detection

Federated model review for MEP coordination conflicts. Issue log generated with resolution recommendations before subcontractor bid packages are distributed.

04

GMP Reconciliation

Post-bid GMP reconciliation incorporating approved VE items, subcontractor buy-outs, and final scope clarifications. Full open-book transparency on all line items.
