Hurricane Roof Straps in Jacksonville: Installation, Inspection, and Insurance Savings

Florida's insurance market has tightened significantly, with premiums rising 20–40% in recent years. Hurricane roof straps — also called roof-to-wall connectors — are one of the documented wind mitigation features that most directly reduce insurance premiums in Florida.

When a wind mitigation inspector finds a qualifying roof-to-wall connection type (clips, single wraps, double wraps, or structural), your insurance company applies a credit that can save $300–$600+ per year. In many cases, the installation cost is recovered in 2–4 years of premium savings.

JK Prestige installs hurricane clips and straps as part of full re-roofing projects, standalone retrofit projects, and FORTIFIED Home certification work. We coordinate the wind mitigation inspection documentation so you can present it to your insurer immediately after installation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does JK Prestige install hurricane roof straps in Jacksonville?
Yes — hurricane clips and roof-to-wall connector installations throughout Jacksonville and Northeast Florida.
How much can hurricane roof straps save on insurance in Jacksonville?
Most Florida homeowners with qualifying roof-to-wall connections save $200–$600+ per year on homeowners insurance. JK Prestige provides documentation for your insurer.
Can hurricane straps be installed without replacing the roof?
Yes — retrofit installations access existing rafters through the attic without requiring a full re-roof.
How much does hurricane strap installation cost in Jacksonville?
Retrofit strap installation typically runs $1,500–$4,000 depending on home size and accessibility. The insurance savings often recover the cost in 2–4 years.
What is a FORTIFIED Roof in Florida?
FORTIFIED Roof is an IBHS certification program that requires enhanced roof deck attachment, sealed roof deck, and wind-rated roofing systems. It qualifies for significant Florida insurance discounts.
