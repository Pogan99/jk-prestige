Our Project Process — 5 Steps

A consistent delivery process adapted to each county's permit environment and construction market.

County-Specific Permit Research

Each Northeast Florida county has distinct plan review timelines, local code amendments, and building department capacity. JK Prestige researches the specific county's current review backlog and fee schedule before developing the project schedule, preventing optimistic permit assumptions that cascade into schedule overruns.

Preconstruction Budget & Value Engineering

Detailed Schedule of Values issued within 48 hours of receiving construction documents. Value engineering alternatives presented before permit submission, when design changes cost hundreds of dollars rather than the tens of thousands they cost after mobilization.

Subcontractor Mobilization

JK Prestige's pre-qualified subcontractor network spans all of Northeast Florida. For projects in Nassau, Flagler, or Clay counties, we identify local subcontractors who can supplement our Jacksonville-based MEP partners, reducing travel time premiums without sacrificing quality standards.

Construction & Pull-Planning

Weekly pull-plan sessions with foremen and subcontractor superintendents establish the 3-week lookahead schedule. Constraint identification at 21+ days in advance allows recovery actions before constraints become schedule delays. JK Prestige's principal walks the site daily.

Final Inspections & CO

County-specific final inspection scheduling, coordinated with the applicable building department's inspection portal. Close-out documentation package delivered within 30 days of CO, including as-builts, O&M manuals, warranties, and lien waivers from all subcontractors and suppliers.
