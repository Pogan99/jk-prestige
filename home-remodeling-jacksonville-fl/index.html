Frequently Asked Questions — Home Remodeling in Jacksonville

How much does home remodeling cost in Jacksonville?

Costs vary by scope. Kitchen remodels run $25,000-$80,000; bathroom remodels $15,000-$45,000; room additions $150-$250 per square foot. We provide detailed written estimates within 48 hours of the site visit.

How do I choose a home remodeling contractor in Jacksonville?

Look for a licensed Florida contractor with verifiable local experience, transparent written estimates, and references from Jacksonville area projects. Ask about their permit record and whether they have any outstanding licensing complaints with the DBPR.

What are the most popular home remodeling projects in Jacksonville?

Kitchen and bathroom remodels consistently rank highest in terms of frequency and return on investment. Room additions, particularly master suites and Florida rooms, are also popular among Jacksonville homeowners who want more space without moving.

Do I need permits for home remodeling in Jacksonville?

Structural work, plumbing modifications, electrical changes, and additions all require permits. Cosmetic work — paint, flooring, replacing fixtures in kind — typically does not. We advise on permit requirements during consultation and pull all necessary permits.

How long does a home remodel take in Jacksonville?

A bathroom remodel takes 4-8 weeks; a kitchen remodel 6-12 weeks; a room addition 3-6 months. Full home remodels can take 6-12 months depending on scope. We establish a realistic schedule before construction begins.
