Soffit and Fascia Repair in Jacksonville: What's Really Causing the Damage

In Jacksonville's humid climate, soffit and fascia are among the most maintenance-intensive elements of a home. Overflowing gutters saturate fascia boards. Roof deck overhang moisture migrates into soffit panels. Once rot starts, it spreads faster than most homeowners expect.

JK Prestige approaches soffit and fascia repair correctly: remove the damaged section, inspect the underlying rafter tails and roof deck edge for moisture intrusion, treat or replace as needed, then install new material with proper flashing to prevent recurrence.

Many soffit and fascia repairs in Florida are covered by homeowners insurance following storm damage. JK Prestige documents damage for insurance claims as part of the repair process.
