Salon Buildout in Jacksonville: Plumbing, Ventilation, and Regulatory Requirements

Salon buildouts look simple but involve more plumbing than most restaurant buildouts. Every shampoo bowl needs a hot and cold water supply and a drain. Pedicure chairs need individual plumbing connections. A 10-chair salon might have 20+ individual plumbing connections running through the floor.

JK Prestige manages the full salon buildout from permit submission through plumbing rough-in inspection, electrical, flooring, cabinetry, and final punch list. We understand the sequence — and the common surprises in commercial tenant spaces.

Florida State Board of Cosmetology regulations require specific ventilation, restroom access, and facility standards for licensed salons. JK Prestige builds to the regulatory requirements so your cosmetology license application goes smoothly.

Does JK Prestige do salon buildouts in Jacksonville?

What permits are required for a salon buildout in Jacksonville?
Building permits for interior construction, plumbing permits for shampoo bowl and pedicure chair connections, and electrical permits. JK Prestige manages all permits.
How much does a salon buildout cost in Jacksonville?
Salon buildouts typically run $50–$120/sq ft depending on number of stations, plumbing connections, and finish level.
How long does a salon buildout take?
Most salon buildouts take 4–8 weeks from permit approval to occupancy.
What ventilation is required for a nail salon in Florida?
Florida requires ventilation systems in nail salons to control chemical fumes. JK Prestige installs code-compliant ventilation systems.
