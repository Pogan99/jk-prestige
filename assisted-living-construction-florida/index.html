Assisted Living Construction Florida

JK Prestige Constructor Corp is Florida's most trusted senior living construction firm — a licensed Florida general contractor with deep expertise in assisted living facilities (ALFs), memory care units, and skilled nursing facilities (SNFs). Preferred by developers and healthcare operators across Florida, we build to AHCA standards and OSHA guidelines, ensuring every project delivers the regulatory compliance, infection control design, and operational functionality required to achieve licensure. With 500+ projects completed, 94 verified 5-star reviews, and 0 recordable OSHA incidents in 2024, our track record speaks for itself.
