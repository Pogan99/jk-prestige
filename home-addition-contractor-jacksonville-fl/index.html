Additions Done Right: What Separates a Good Project from a Great One

Home additions in Jacksonville require more than framing and drywall. The connection point between your existing home and the new addition is where most problems originate — roof transitions that don't waterproof properly, foundation ties that aren't engineered for the load, and interior transitions that never quite look right. We've fixed enough poorly executed additions over the years to know exactly where other contractors cut corners.

Our process starts with a structural assessment of your existing home before we ever finalize an addition design. Florida's soil conditions, particularly in areas like Mandarin or along the St. Johns River corridor, require careful foundation evaluation. We don't assume — we verify.

We also handle all Duval County permitting as part of our standard service. Addition projects require architectural plans, structural engineering review, and multiple inspections. Many homeowners are surprised by how involved the permitting process is — we manage it completely so you don't have to navigate it alone.

The result is a home addition that blends so naturally with your existing structure that visitors can't tell where the original house ended and the new construction began. That's the level of craftsmanship we bring to every project in Ponte Vedra, Orange Park, Fleming Island, and throughout the Jacksonville metro.
