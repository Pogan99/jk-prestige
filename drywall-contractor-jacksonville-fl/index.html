Frequently Asked Questions

What drywall finish level should I specify?

Level 4 is standard for most painted interior walls. Level 5 — a skim coat finish — is specified for rooms with gloss paint, raking light, or high-end finishes where imperfections would show.

How much does drywall installation cost in Jacksonville?

Drywall hanging and finishing for new construction runs $2.50 to $4.50 per square foot of board installed, depending on ceiling height, finish level, and layout complexity.

Do you repair drywall after water damage?

Yes. We cut out damaged sections, treat for mold if indicated, replace with moisture-resistant board, and finish to match existing walls.

Can you match existing drywall texture?

Yes. We match orange peel, knockdown, skip-trowel, and smooth finishes. Texture matching on repairs is part of every drywall patch scope.

How long does drywall take for a home addition?

A 400 to 600 sq ft addition typically takes 3 to 5 days for hang, tape, and three-coat finish with drying time between coats.
