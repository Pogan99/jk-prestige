Florida Room Additions in Jacksonville — What You Need to Know

Florida rooms are one of the most uniquely regional home improvement projects available to Jacksonville homeowners. No other addition type captures the specific pleasures and challenges of living in Northeast Florida — the ability to enjoy outdoor air without insects, the desire for shade during humid summer afternoons, and the year-round mild weather that makes an indoor-outdoor transition genuinely livable. JK Prestige has built Florida rooms in established Jacksonville neighborhoods for years and brings both technical skill and genuine local knowledge to every project.

Structurally, a Florida room addition must tie into your existing home's roof and wall systems in a way that is both watertight and meets Florida Building Code wind load requirements. The connection between the addition's roof and the existing structure is the most critical detail — poorly executed connections are the leading cause of water intrusion in Florida room additions. We use proper flashing, sealing, and structural connections on every project.

Screened rooms require framing that can handle Florida's wind pressure. Standard aluminum screen room systems sold as kits are often undersized for Jacksonville's wind zone requirements. We build Florida rooms with proper structural calculations, engineered framing where required, and screen systems rated for the local wind environment. Impact-rated screening is available for maximum protection in coastal and near-coastal locations.

Glass-enclosed sunrooms require more detailed permitting than screened rooms. Impact-rated or protected glazing is required in most Duval County wind zones. HVAC must be extended to condition the space or a dedicated mini-split system installed. Proper insulation of the roof and knee walls prevents excessive heat gain during Jacksonville's summer months.

Florida room additions range from approximately $25,000 for a basic screened room to $65,000 or more for a fully conditioned glass-enclosed sunroom with premium materials. Financing is available through our third-party lending partners.

Frequently Asked Questions — Florida Room Additions in Jacksonville

How much does a Florida room addition cost in Jacksonville?

Florida room additions in Jacksonville range from approximately $25,000 for a basic screened room to $65,000 or more for a fully glass-enclosed, conditioned sunroom. The biggest cost drivers are size, whether the room is screened or fully glazed, roofing type, and HVAC requirements.

Do screened rooms require permits in Jacksonville?

Yes. All structural additions, including screened rooms attached to the home, require building permits in Jacksonville. JK Prestige handles all permit applications and inspections.

Will a glass Florida room be too hot in Jacksonville summers?

Only if it is built without proper insulated glazing, roof insulation, and climate control. We design glass-enclosed sunrooms with insulated glass panels, proper roof insulation, and HVAC conditioning so the room is comfortable year-round.

Can I add a Florida room to a screened porch I already have?

In many cases yes, depending on the existing structure's condition and load capacity. We assess existing structures and determine whether they can be upgraded or whether a full rebuild is more practical.

How long does a Florida room addition take?

Most Florida room additions take 2 to 4 months from permit approval depending on size and complexity. Screened rooms generally move faster than fully enclosed glass rooms.
