GAF Golden Pledge in Jacksonville: What It Is, What It Covers, and Who Can Offer It

The GAF Golden Pledge is not available from every roofing contractor — only GAF Master Elite contractors are authorized to offer it. Master Elite status requires demonstrated workmanship quality, financial stability, ongoing training, and a clean complaint history. JK Prestige has earned and maintained Master Elite status since we entered the roofing market.

What separates the Golden Pledge from standard roofing warranties: standard material warranties cover manufacturing defects in the shingles themselves. The Golden Pledge adds 25 years of workmanship coverage — meaning if an installation error causes a leak in year 12, it's covered.

At resale, a home with an active transferable GAF Golden Pledge warranty is a documented advantage. Buyers and their agents know what it means — and appraisers in some markets recognize the premium.
