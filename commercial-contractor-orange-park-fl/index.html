Our Orange Park Commercial Process — 5 Steps

Jurisdiction-aware permitting and self-perform delivery for Clay County's primary commercial hub.

Parcel Research & Permit Authority Identification

Before submitting any permit application for an Orange Park commercial project, JK Prestige verifies the parcel's jurisdiction — Town of Orange Park or unincorporated Clay County — through the Clay County Property Appraiser's database. This step prevents the costly mistake of submitting to the wrong building department, which invalidates the application and adds 2–4 weeks to the permit timeline.

Preconstruction Budget & Existing Conditions

Orange Park's commercial building stock includes significant 1970s–1990s vintage construction that may require pre-renovation asbestos and lead-based paint surveys. JK Prestige coordinates hazardous materials testing as part of the preconstruction scope for renovation projects on older commercial buildings, incorporating abatement cost and timeline into the project budget before contract execution.

Mobilization & Self-Perform Trades

JK Prestige self-performs framing and drywall on Orange Park commercial projects — the critical-path trades that determine whether a Blanding Blvd retail or medical office buildout hits its scheduled opening date. Self-performance at 15 miles from Jacksonville headquarters eliminates the subcontractor availability uncertainty that affects contractor responsiveness in Clay County.

Construction, Supervision & Weekly OAC

Daily site supervision by JK Prestige's principal. Weekly OAC meetings on-site (not by video call from Jacksonville). Pull-planning sessions with all trade foremen establish the 3-week lookahead and constraint log. Constraint identification at 21+ days in advance allows recovery actions before they impact the critical path.

Final Inspections, CO & Close-Out

Final inspections through the correct building department (Town of Orange Park or Clay County), fire marshal coordination, and utility release for CO. Complete close-out documentation package delivered within 30 days of CO, including as-built drawings, O&M manuals, equipment warranties, and lien waivers from all subcontractors and suppliers.
