Frequently Asked Questions

What does GAF Master Elite certification mean for me as a homeowner?

GAF Master Elite is awarded to the top 3% of roofing contractors nationally. It means we're factory-trained, background-checked, and licensed. More importantly, it unlocks the GAF Golden Pledge warranty — a 25-year labor-and-materials warranty backed by GAF, not just us. Uncertified contractors cannot offer this warranty regardless of which materials they use.

How long does a roof replacement take in Jacksonville?

Most standard residential replacements in Jacksonville are completed in one to two days. Larger homes, complex rooflines, or commercial projects may take longer. We give you a written timeline with your estimate and keep you informed of any weather-related delays.

Do I need to be home during the roofing inspection?

For the exterior inspection, you do not need to be present — though we prefer it so we can walk you through findings in person. For the interior attic inspection, someone should be available to provide access. We schedule inspections around your availability and typically complete them in 30–60 minutes.
