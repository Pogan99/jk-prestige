Frequently Asked Questions

How much does stucco cost in Jacksonville FL?

Stucco installation in Jacksonville typically ranges from $8 to $14 per square foot installed, depending on surface complexity and finish type. Repairs are quoted per job scope.

How long does stucco last in Florida?

Properly installed stucco can last 50 years or more. Florida's UV exposure and humidity require quality waterproof coatings and proper detailing at windows and trim.

Can stucco be applied over existing siding?

In most cases stucco requires a proper substrate — typically sheathing with weather barrier and metal lath. Our team will assess the existing wall assembly before recommending an approach.

Do you repair stucco cracks?

Yes. We repair hairline cracks, structural cracks, and areas with delamination or moisture intrusion. We match existing texture and color as closely as possible.

Are you licensed for stucco work in Florida?

Yes. JK Prestige Constructor holds a Florida Certified General Contractor (CGC) license covering all exterior finish work including stucco.
