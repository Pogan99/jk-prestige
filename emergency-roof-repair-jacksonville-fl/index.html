Emergency Roof Repair FAQ

What should I do if my roof is actively leaking right now?

Call (904) 944-0278 immediately. While waiting: place buckets to catch water, move furniture and valuables, and if water is collecting near electrical fixtures, flip the breaker for that area. Do not go onto the roof yourself during or after a storm.

How fast can JK Prestige respond to an emergency?

Response time depends on the scale of the weather event and simultaneous calls. We triage by severity — active water intrusion with structural or interior damage risk is prioritized. We give an honest ETA when you call and keep you updated. We do not abandon calls once accepted.

Is emergency tarp installation covered by homeowner's insurance?

In most cases, yes. Emergency mitigation (tarping) is a covered loss mitigation expense under most Florida homeowner's policies when the underlying damage is from a covered peril. We document tarp installation with photographs and provide an itemized invoice for insurance submission.

What is involved in a proper emergency tarp installation?

A proper tarp covers the damaged area with at least 4 feet of overlap on each side. It is secured with cap nails and battens on the top edge so it cannot lift in wind. Improperly installed tarps blow off in the first wind event. We install tarps to hold through weather events.
