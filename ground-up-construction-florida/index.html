JK Prestige Constructor Corp is a licensed Florida general contractor specializing in ground-up new construction for commercial, medical, industrial, and institutional clients across the state. Ground-up construction requires a GC with command of every project phase — from geotechnical review and SWPPP implementation through structural system selection, MEP coordination, envelope installation, and the final close-out documentation package that delivers a complete building to ownership. Since 2017, JK Prestige has delivered more than 500 ground-up and major new construction projects in Florida, ranging from single-story medical offices to multi-story commercial towers and large-footprint industrial facilities exceeding 480,000 square feet. Our preconstruction team engages early in the process — reviewing conceptual documents, validating program assumptions against current Jacksonville-area subcontractor pricing, and identifying long-lead procurement items that could threaten your opening schedule. We self-perform framing, drywall, and commercial roofing, which gives us direct schedule control on three of the most schedule-sensitive trades in any ground-up build. Our project delivery includes formal Lean Last Planner scheduling, weekly owner reports, and a documented submittal log that tracks every specification section from issue through approval and installation. Florida developers, corporate facility teams, and institutional owners who need a reliable, licensed GC for their next ground-up project will find in JK Prestige a firm that operates with the transparency, technical depth, and urgency their investment requires.

Ground-Up Construction Phases — What to Expect

A disciplined project sequence with defined milestones and owner reporting at each phase.

1

Preconstruction & Budget Validation

Program review, geotechnical assessment, preliminary budget with system alternatives, long-lead identification, and permit timeline analysis before contract execution.

2

Permit Submission & Pre-Construction Procurement

Building permit submission, concurrent structural steel and mechanical equipment procurement, subcontractor bid-leveling, and award during plan review period to eliminate lead time from the critical path.

3

Site Mobilization & Foundation

Site clearing, earthwork, SWPPP installation, utility rough-in, formwork, and foundation construction. Weekly progress photos and schedule updates delivered to owner and lender.

4

Structure, Envelope & Rough-In

Structural system erection or tilt-up panel construction, envelope installation, MEP rough-in, framing, and drywall. Submittal log maintained and reported weekly.

5

Finish Construction & Certificate of Occupancy

Interior finishes, MEP trim, specialty systems, punch-list completion, close-out documentation package (O&M manuals, as-builts, warranties, lien releases), and CO issuance.
