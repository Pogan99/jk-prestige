What GAF Master Elite Means for Your Roof

The certification requires active compliance with four standards — verified by GAF annually.

Valid Florida Contractor's License

GAF requires a current, unrestricted Florida Roofing Contractor license. This is separately verifiable at myfloridalicense.com. JK Prestige holds a valid Florida license and has held it continuously since 2017.

Adequate Insurance Coverage

GAF requires liability and workers' compensation insurance at specified minimum levels. This protects you from liability if a worker is injured on your property. Uninsured roofing contractors in Florida expose homeowners to direct liability for worker injuries.

Proven Track Record

GAF verifies installation volume, customer satisfaction, and complaint history. Contractors who generate significant complaints or have installation-related warranty claims filed against them lose Master Elite status at renewal. JK Prestige has maintained it since initial certification.

Ongoing Training Completion

GAF Master Elite contractors complete annual training covering new product specifications, installation techniques, and Florida Building Code updates. This training directly affects installation quality and warranty eligibility for each new product line.

GAF Master Elite FAQ — Jacksonville

What does GAF Master Elite certification mean?

Only about 2% of all roofing contractors qualify. Requirements include a valid Florida license, adequate insurance, a proven track record, and completed GAF training. The certification renews annually — it is not awarded once and kept forever.

What GAF warranties are available through a Master Elite contractor?

Master Elite contractors can offer Silver Pledge (15-year non-prorated, materials + labor), Gold Pledge (25-year materials, 10-year labor), and Golden Pledge (25-year non-prorated, materials + labor). All three are transferable. Standard contractors can only offer the base material warranty.

What is the GAF Golden Pledge warranty?

The most comprehensive residential roofing warranty available. Covers both GAF materials and workmanship for up to 25 years on a non-prorated basis. Transferable to subsequent owners. Only available through GAF Master Elite contractors.

How do I verify a contractor is actually GAF Master Elite certified?

Verify at contractor.gaf.com by entering the contractor's name or zip code. If a contractor claims Master Elite but cannot be found in GAF's locator, they are not currently certified. You can also verify our Florida license at myfloridalicense.com.
