Re-Roofing FAQ — Jacksonville

What is the difference between re-roofing and overlay?

Re-roofing (full tear-off) removes all existing material to the deck before installing a new system. Overlay installs new shingles over existing ones without removal. JK Prestige performs full tear-offs only — overlays hide deck damage, void warranties, and are code-limited to two layers.

How long does re-roofing take in Jacksonville?

Shingle re-roofing typically takes 1–3 days. Tile re-roofing takes 3–5 days. Metal roofing takes 2–4 days. Weather delays are possible; we communicate all changes proactively.

Do I need a permit for re-roofing in Duval County?

Yes. A permit is required for all full re-roofing in Duval County and surrounding counties. JK Prestige pulls all required permits before work begins. Unpermitted work can result in failed inspections, insurance claim denials, and required removal and reinstallation.

How much does re-roofing cost in Jacksonville?

Shingle re-roofing on 1,500 sqft typically runs $9,000–$14,000. Metal re-roofing runs $18,000–$32,000. Tile re-roofing runs $18,000–$38,000. Cost factors include roof pitch, layers being removed, deck condition, and material selection.
