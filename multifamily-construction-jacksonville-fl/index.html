Multifamily Construction Jacksonville, FL

JK Prestige Constructor Corp delivers ground-up multifamily construction for developers, REITs, and housing authorities across Jacksonville and Northeast Florida. Since 2017, our team has executed apartment complexes, mixed-use podium structures, and master-planned townhome communities — managing the full construction lifecycle from civil coordination and foundation work through vertical structure, MEP rough-in, interior finishes, and certificate of occupancy.

Northeast Florida's multifamily market demands a GC that understands high-density wood-frame construction, post-tension podium decks, and the permitting intricacies of Duval County's Development Services Division. Our project managers carry active Florida GC licenses, maintain OSHA 30 certifications, and have delivered projects ranging from 48-unit garden-style communities to 280-unit podium towers. We work directly with ownership groups, third-party developers, and equity partners who require transparent reporting, schedule accountability, and zero change-order surprises.
