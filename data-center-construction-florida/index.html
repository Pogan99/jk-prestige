Data Center Construction Florida

JK Prestige Constructor Corp delivers mission critical facility construction for Tier I through Tier III data centers, colocation facilities, and edge computing installations across Florida. Jacksonville's position as a major fiber interconnect hub, combined with Florida's business-friendly tax environment and proximity to the Caribbean cable landing stations, continues to attract hyperscale and enterprise data center investment to Northeast Florida — and JK Prestige is positioned to deliver these technically demanding facilities on schedule.

Data center construction requires a GC that understands concurrent maintainability, N+1 and 2N redundancy configurations, and the sequencing complexity of commissioning high-power electrical systems, precision cooling infrastructure, and fire suppression systems in the same facility simultaneously. Our project teams coordinate with MEP engineers on generator paralleling, UPS system installation, overhead busway, CRAC and CRAH unit placement, hot/cold aisle containment systems, and raised access floor infrastructure — delivering facilities that meet Uptime Institute tier criteria and are ready for owner testing and commissioning.
