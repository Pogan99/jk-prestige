Dental Office Construction in Jacksonville: What It Takes to Get It Right

Dental office construction has specific requirements that separate it from standard commercial fit-outs: nitrous oxide and vacuum plumbing manifolds, dental unit power and water connections, X-ray room shielding, sterilization area plumbing, and cabinetry built to manufacturer specifications.

JK Prestige coordinates with your dental equipment supplier and interior designer to ensure rough-in locations, power capacity, and plumbing manifolds are precisely where your equipment requires them. We've built dental operatories throughout Northeast Florida and understand the sequence that gets your practice open on time.

Florida requires a licensed contractor for all dental office construction. Working with an unlicensed contractor risks code violations that can delay your certificate of occupancy and your practice opening.
