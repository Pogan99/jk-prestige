What Actually Drives Custom Home Costs in Jacksonville FL in 2026

In 2026, building a custom home in Jacksonville FL typically runs $200–$420+ per square foot depending on where you build and how you build. Those numbers reflect total construction cost — not land, not financing, not furniture. Lot costs in Jacksonville range from $30,000 for a standard Duval County lot to $500,000+ for premium Ponte Vedra or Nocatee lots.

The biggest cost drivers beyond finishes: site conditions (how much site work is required), floor plan complexity (rectangles cost less than angles), ceiling heights (volume costs money to condition), and permit timeline (St. Johns County runs longer than Duval County, which affects carrying costs).

According to the National Association of Home Builders, material costs represent approximately 50% of a custom home's construction cost, with labor at 25–30% and overhead/profit at 15–20%. In Florida's post-hurricane market, insurance and compliance costs add another 5–8%.
