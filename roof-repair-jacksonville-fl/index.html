Roof Repair Services in Jacksonville

Every type of roof repair performed by certified crews — shingle, tile, metal, and flat roof systems.

Shingle Repair & Replacement

Individual shingles replaced with matching product and color where possible. Lifted and cracked shingles re-nailed and sealed. Missing shingles replaced before water intrusion causes deck damage. Storm damage documentation included.

Flashing Repair & Replacement

Step flashing, valley flashing, chimney flashing, and drip edge replacement. Pipe boot replacement with EPDM pipe boots sealed with roofing cement. Flashing failure is the most common cause of localized roof leaks in Jacksonville.

Ridge Cap & Hip Repair

Ridge cap shingles replaced with matching material. Tile roof ridge and hip mortar repointed or replaced. Ventilated ridge cap installed where existing vented ridge has failed or was never properly installed.

Gutters & Soffit Repair

Gutters re-hung and re-pitched for proper drainage. Soffit and fascia board replacement where water infiltration has caused rot. Gutter guard installation. Drip edge correction to ensure water routes properly into gutters.

Roof Repair FAQ — Jacksonville

How much does roof repair cost in Jacksonville FL?

Minor repairs (1–5 shingles, flashing resealed) run $200–$600. Mid-size repairs (valley, ridge cap, pipe boot) run $400–$1,200. Larger storm damage repairs run $800–$3,500. Written estimate after free inspection.

What is the most common cause of roof leaks in Jacksonville?

Failed or improperly installed pipe boot flashings, separated valley flashing, lifted or missing shingles after wind events, cracked ridge cap mortar on tile roofs, and failed step flashing at wall intersections. Leaks rarely originate from mid-field shingle failure on roofs under 20 years old.

Should I repair or replace my roof?

If your roof is under 15 years old and less than 25% shows damage, repair is almost always the better financial decision. If your roof is over 20 years old with multiple leak points or widespread granule loss, replacement is often more cost-effective. We present both options in writing after inspection.

How quickly can JK Prestige respond to a roof repair?

For standard repair requests, we schedule inspection within 1–3 business days and complete most repairs within the same week. For emergencies (active leaking, storm damage), we offer 24/7 emergency tarp response to prevent further water intrusion.

Will homeowner's insurance cover roof repair?

Storm damage (wind, hail, fallen trees) is typically covered. Normal wear and aging is not. We provide detailed damage documentation including photographs, measurements, and written scope for direct submission to your adjuster.
