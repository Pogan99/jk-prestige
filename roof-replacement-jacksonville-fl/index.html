Roof Replacement FAQ

How do I know if I need a full replacement vs. a repair?

If your roof is under 15 years old and damage is isolated to one area, repair is often the right call. If your roof is 20+ years old, has widespread granule loss, multiple active leaks, or structural deck damage, replacement is typically more cost-effective long-term. Our free inspection gives you an honest answer — we'll tell you which option makes sense for your situation, not just the higher-ticket option.

Can I roof over my existing shingles to save money?

Florida building code permits one re-roof over existing shingles in some cases, but most manufacturer warranties — including GAF's Golden Pledge — require a full tear-off for full coverage. A re-roof also hides deck damage that, if left unaddressed, will shorten the life of your new roof significantly. We advise full tear-off on every replacement and price it accordingly.

How much does a roof replacement cost in Jacksonville FL?

Most residential roof replacements in Jacksonville range from $8,000 to $20,000+ depending on square footage, pitch, material selection, and deck condition. We provide itemized written estimates so you understand exactly what you're paying for. Financing options are available through our lending partners for qualified homeowners.
