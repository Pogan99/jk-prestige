JK Prestige Constructor Corp delivers high-performance warehouse and distribution center construction across Florida for logistics operators, e-commerce retailers, third-party logistics providers, and industrial developers. Based in Jacksonville, FL 32202 — a major Southeast logistics hub at the intersection of I-95, I-10, and the Port of Jacksonville — we understand the operational requirements that drive modern warehouse design. Our project experience spans bulk distribution, cold storage, cross-dock, last-mile fulfillment, and manufacturing facilities from 50,000 to over 480,000 square feet. We self-perform roofing and concrete-adjacent trades, providing direct schedule control on the envelope and slab — the two most schedule-critical elements of any warehouse project. Our tilt-up concrete construction expertise covers panel design coordination with the structural engineer of record, casting bed layout, brace engineering, and crane planning for efficient panel erection. ESFR and CMSA fire suppression system coordination, LED site and warehouse lighting, dock equipment procurement, and final concrete flatwork are managed under a single contract, giving industrial developers and property managers a single accountable GC for the entire scope. From site clearing through certificate of occupancy, JK Prestige delivers Florida warehouse projects on the accelerated schedules that today's logistics and industrial real estate markets demand.
