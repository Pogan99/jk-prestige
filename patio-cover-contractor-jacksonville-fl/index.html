Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit for a patio cover in Jacksonville?

Most attached patio covers and screen enclosures require a permit from the City of Jacksonville or St. Johns County. JK Prestige Constructor pulls all permits and coordinates inspections.

How much does a patio cover cost in Jacksonville FL?

A basic insulated aluminum cover for a 20x20 patio runs $8,000 to $14,000 installed including permits. Pergolas and screen enclosures vary by size and materials.

What is the best patio cover for Florida weather?

Insulated aluminum panel systems perform well in Florida — they block UV, reduce heat gain, and resist humidity. Screen enclosures are popular for keeping insects out while maintaining airflow.

Can you build over an existing concrete patio?

Yes. Most of our patio covers are installed over existing concrete slabs with new footings anchored to the slab edge or to the ground beyond the slab perimeter.

How long does patio cover construction take?

A standard 20x20 cover typically takes 1 to 2 weeks from permit approval. Screen enclosures can take 2 to 3 weeks depending on scope.
