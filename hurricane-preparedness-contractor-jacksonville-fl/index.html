Frequently Asked Questions

What is a wind mitigation inspection in Florida?

A wind mitigation inspection documents your home's hurricane-resistant features (roof shape, roof covering, roof deck attachment, opening protection) for your insurance carrier. Documented features typically result in premium discounts.

Can I retrofit hurricane straps on an existing home?

Yes. Retrofit strap systems can be installed in most existing homes through the attic. JK Prestige Constructor installs code-compliant strap systems and provides documentation for wind mitigation reports.

How much does hurricane hardening cost in Jacksonville?

A basic hardening package (straps + impact front door + storm shutters) typically runs $5,000 to $15,000. Full impact window replacement for a home adds $12,000 to $25,000.

When should I start hurricane prep before storm season?

Florida hurricane season runs June 1 to November 30. We recommend scheduling hardening work between January and April to avoid permit backlogs and contractor shortages that occur in spring.

Do impact windows qualify for Florida insurance discounts?

Yes. Impact-rated openings are a major factor in wind mitigation discounts. Savings vary by carrier but frequently exceed the cost of installation over a 10-year period.
