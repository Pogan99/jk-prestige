Site Work Contractor Jacksonville, FL

JK Prestige Constructor Corp provides comprehensive site work services for commercial, industrial, and multifamily development across Jacksonville and Northeast Florida. Successful ground-up construction begins with a properly prepared site — and that means earthwork, grading, and utility installation executed to plan, in compliance with FDEP stormwater regulations, SJRWMD permitting requirements, and the erosion control mandates that govern Florida construction sites.

Northeast Florida's high water table, sandy soils, and frequent rainfall create site work challenges that require local expertise. Our crews have mobilized on undisturbed lots, former agricultural land, and infill urban parcels across Duval, St. Johns, Clay, and Nassau counties. We manage site clearing, mass grading, fine grading, underground utility installation, stormwater pond construction, and SWPPP implementation — keeping regulatory inspectors and environmental agencies satisfied throughout the project lifecycle. When site work is performed correctly from day one, vertical construction proceeds without the delays that corrupt scheduling and drain contingency budgets.

Site Work Services

Complete site preparation from clearing through final grade certification.

🏗

Site Clearing & Demolition

Vegetation clearing, tree removal, stump grinding, and existing structure demolition. Debris disposal and site clean-up in compliance with Duval County environmental requirements.

🌵

Mass Grading & Earthwork

Cut and fill operations, import and export of engineered fill, compaction testing coordination, subgrade preparation, and final grade certification for pad-ready sites.

🔧

Underground Utility Installation

Sanitary sewer, water main, storm drainage, and dry utility conduit installation. JEA coordination for water and sewer connections. Pressure testing, video inspection, and FDEP reporting.

🌊

Erosion Control & SWPPP

Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan implementation and maintenance. Silt fence installation, inlet protection, sediment basins, turbidity barriers, and weekly BMP inspection reporting for FDEP compliance.

💧

Stormwater Pond & Infrastructure

Retention and detention pond excavation, pond liner systems, control structure installation, and outfall piping. SJRWMD permit compliance and as-built survey coordination.
