Multi-Trade Contractor Jacksonville, FL

JK Prestige Constructor Corp is one of Northeast Florida's few true multi-trade general contractors — a firm that self-performs framing, drywall, and roofing with its own licensed crews rather than subcontracting these core trades to outside firms. This multi-trade capability is a structural competitive advantage that translates into measurable benefits for every client: no subcontractor markup on core scope, no scheduling dependency on sub availability, no finger-pointing when work sequencing creates defects at trade interfaces.

In the current Northeast Florida construction market, subcontractor availability and pricing remain volatile. A GC that brokers every trade faces constant scheduling risk and margin compression that they ultimately pass to owners. JK Prestige built its multi-trade model specifically to insulate clients from this market volatility. Our in-house framing crews erect structural and non-structural systems to the tolerances that MEP rough-in and finish trades require. Our drywall crews perform framing, board, tape, and finish work under a single chain of accountability. Our GAF Master Elite roofing crews execute from deck preparation through final inspection — without hand-offs, warranty gaps, or diffuse accountability.
