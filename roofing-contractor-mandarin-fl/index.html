Roofing Services in Mandarin, Jacksonville FL

The roofing needs in Mandarin are varied: older homes often need full structural assessment before tile can be added, and many homes from the 1980s and 1990s are approaching roof end-of-life. JK Prestige Constructor Corp is headquartered in Jacksonville and serves Mandarin as one of our primary neighborhoods. Mandarin Roofing FAQ

Do you serve Julington Creek Plantation?

Yes. Julington Creek Plantation falls in both Duval and St. Johns County depending on the specific subdivision. We serve the entire area and are familiar with both county's permit requirements.

My Mandarin home was built in the 1980s. What should I expect for a roof replacement?

1980s Duval County homes often have decking issues (older plywood or board decking that may need partial replacement), and many were built with 3-tab shingles over minimal underlayment. Expect a full tear-off with likely 10–20% deck repair. We document all deck findings with photography before proceeding.

How do you handle large tree debris on Mandarin roofs?

Mandarin's mature live oaks regularly deposit limbs on roofs. We assess structural integrity before beginning any work, remove debris, and photograph all damage for insurance documentation. We coordinate with tree removal services if needed.
