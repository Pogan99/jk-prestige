Education Construction Jacksonville, FL

JK Prestige Constructor Corp is Northeast Florida's leading education construction firm — delivering SREF-compliant K-12 schools, charter campuses, and higher education buildings for Jacksonville's growing educational market. With 500+ projects completed, 94 verified 5-star reviews, and 0 recordable OSHA incidents in 2024, we are preferred by school boards, charter operators, and universities across the region. School construction in Florida carries a unique regulatory burden: SREF requirements layer on top of the Florida Building Code, and non-compliance risks delayed occupancy and withheld state funding. All work is permitted through Duval County Building Services and adheres to OSHA standards. We are AGC-affiliated and deliver 48-hour estimates.

Our project managers have navigated SREF plan review, School Board of Duval County procurement requirements, and state-mandated testing and inspection protocols on multiple campuses. We understand the sequencing demands of educational construction — summer break windows for occupied renovations, AV, security, and classroom technology coordination, and the public accountability that comes with taxpayer-funded school construction. JK Prestige delivers education facilities that pass inspection and open on the first day of school. Jacksonville's commercial market generated $2.8B in permits in 2024, with education a significant and growing sector.

"Education construction in Florida has a hard deadline: the first day of school. SREF compliance, occupied-campus phasing, and technology coordination have to be managed in parallel. We've done it on multiple Northeast Florida campuses — and we've never missed an opening date." — J. Karras, Founder & CEO, JK Prestige Constructor Corp
