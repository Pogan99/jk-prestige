About This Service

JK Prestige Constructor installs impact-rated windows and doors throughout Jacksonville and Northeast Florida. We handle permit-ready installations for both replacement projects and new construction, using products that meet Florida's High-Velocity Hurricane Zone requirements where applicable.

Common projects include whole-home window replacement for energy savings, impact glass upgrades for insurance discounts, and front door and sliding glass door replacements as part of larger renovation scopes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit to replace windows in Jacksonville?

Yes. Window replacements in Jacksonville require a permit. JK Prestige Constructor handles permit applications and inspections as part of every project.

Will impact windows lower my homeowner's insurance in Florida?

Many Florida insurers offer discounts for impact-rated windows and doors. Savings vary by carrier and coverage. We provide product NOA (Notice of Acceptance) documentation your insurer requires.

How much do impact windows cost in Jacksonville?

Impact window replacement for a typical 3-bedroom home in Jacksonville ranges from $12,000 to $22,000 depending on window count, size, and frame material. We provide detailed line-item quotes.

How long does window replacement take?

A whole-home window replacement typically takes 2 to 4 days for installation, plus permit lead time of 1 to 3 weeks.

Can you replace windows as part of a larger renovation?

Yes. We frequently bundle window and door replacement into kitchen, bath, and whole-home renovation contracts to reduce mobilization costs and streamline permitting.
