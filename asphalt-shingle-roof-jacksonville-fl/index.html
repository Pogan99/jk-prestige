Asphalt Shingle Roofing in Jacksonville: What to Know Before You Replace

Asphalt shingles are the most widely installed roofing material in Jacksonville — and for good reason. They offer the best cost-to-performance ratio for Florida homeowners who plan to be in their home for 15–25 years. The key is installation quality.

JK Prestige installs shingles to Florida Building Code requirements, which are among the strictest in the country: 6-nail fastening pattern in high-wind zones, proper starter strip at eaves and rakes, synthetic underlayment, and continuous ridge vent where possible.

The GAF Golden Pledge warranty is available on qualifying shingle installations — covering both materials and workmanship for the life of your home. Only GAF Master Elite contractors like JK Prestige can offer it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best shingle for a Jacksonville FL roof?
GAF Timberline HDZ or HDZ RS architectural shingles are among the most popular and best-performing options for Jacksonville. For maximum longevity, Class 4 impact-resistant shingles are worth the premium.
How long do shingles last in Jacksonville FL?
Standard architectural shingles last 18–22 years in Jacksonville's climate. Class 4 impact-resistant shingles reach 22–26 years with proper ventilation and maintenance.
Does JK Prestige install GAF shingles in Jacksonville?
Yes — JK Prestige is a GAF Master Elite contractor and installs the full GAF product line, including Timberline HDZ, Camelot II, and Woodland impact-resistant shingles.
How much does a shingle roof replacement cost in Jacksonville?
Residential shingle roof replacement typically runs $7,500–$18,000 in Jacksonville depending on size and pitch. Written estimate within 48 hours.
Does a new shingle roof require a permit in Jacksonville?
Yes — all residential roof replacements require a Duval County permit. JK Prestige manages the full permit process.
