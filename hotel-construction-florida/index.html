Hotel Construction in Florida: Process, Cost, and What to Expect

Florida's hospitality market generates significant hotel construction activity — new properties in Jacksonville, Orlando, Tampa, and coastal markets, plus major PIP renovation programs as franchise brands update their portfolios.

JK Prestige brings the same self-perform capability and fixed-price commitment to hotel construction that we apply to every project type. Hotel projects have hard opening dates — franchise agreements and marketing commitments depend on it. We build to those dates.

The critical path on hotel construction: early MEP rough-in coordination, guestroom bathroom sequence, elevator installation timing, and soft good coordination with the FF&E supplier. JK Prestige manages all of it under one contract.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does JK Prestige build hotels in Florida?
Yes — new hotel construction, PIP renovations, and adaptive reuse conversions throughout Florida.
What hotel brands has JK Prestige worked with?
JK Prestige works with owners and developers to meet franchise brand standards for major hospitality brands. Contact us for current brand experience.
How much does hotel construction cost in Florida?
New hotel construction in Florida typically runs $150,000–$250,000+ per key (room) depending on brand, market, and amenity level.
How long does a hotel construction project take?
New hotel development typically takes 18–30 months from groundbreaking to opening. PIP renovations range from 2–8 months.
Can JK Prestige build hotels in Northeast Florida?
Yes — JK Prestige serves the entire Northeast Florida market and has capacity for hospitality projects throughout the region.
