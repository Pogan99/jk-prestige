Auto Shop Construction in Florida: Technical Requirements and Build Process

Auto repair shop construction requires specialized knowledge that most commercial contractors lack. In-ground lift installations require precise concrete pit construction to manufacturer tolerances. Floor drains must meet Florida environmental requirements for oil-water separation.

JK Prestige has built automotive service facilities in Northeast Florida. We understand the trades sequence — concrete work, underground plumbing, electrical rough-in, and equipment installation — and manage it precisely so your lifts go in at the right time and your shop opens on schedule.

Environmental compliance is critical for auto shop construction in Florida. Oil-water separator systems, floor drain configuration, and hazardous material storage are all regulated by the Florida Department of Environmental Protection.
