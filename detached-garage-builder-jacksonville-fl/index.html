Detached Garage Construction in Jacksonville: What You Need to Know

A detached garage is one of the most practical additions you can make to a Jacksonville property — providing vehicle storage, workshop space, or extra storage in a climate that rusts cars and degrades outdoor equipment.

Florida Building Code requires permits for any detached structure with electrical, plumbing, or mechanical systems, and in most cases even for standard storage buildings over a certain size. JK Prestige handles Duval, Clay, and St. Johns county permits as standard practice.

We build detached garages on concrete slab foundations with framing matched to your home's style, roofing that meets Florida's wind-load requirements, and electrical service sized for your actual needs — not the minimum.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit to build a detached garage in Jacksonville FL?
Yes — any detached structure with electrical, plumbing, or mechanical systems requires a permit. Even a basic storage structure over 200 sq ft typically requires a permit. JK Prestige manages all permitting.
How much does a detached garage cost in Jacksonville FL?
Standard 2-car detached garages typically run $35,000–$60,000 fully finished with electrical. Larger custom builds with HVAC run higher.
Can JK Prestige build a garage apartment (ADU) in Jacksonville?
Yes — detached garages with finished living space above are permitted as ADUs in most Jacksonville areas. Free estimate available.
How long does it take to build a detached garage?
Most standard detached garages are completed in 4–8 weeks from permit approval.
Can JK Prestige match the garage to my existing home's exterior?
Yes — we match siding, roofing material, trim, and window style to your home's existing architecture.
