Interior Renovation in Jacksonville — Getting It Right

Interior renovation projects are among the most personal construction work a contractor does. The choices made inside your home — cabinetry finishes, tile patterns, flooring materials, trim profiles — reflect your aesthetic and your lifestyle in ways that exterior work rarely does. JK Prestige approaches every interior renovation with the understanding that getting the details right matters as much as getting the structure right.

Jacksonville's climate creates specific considerations even for interior renovation work. Bathrooms require proper moisture management — behind-tile waterproofing membranes, properly sloped shower pans, and ventilation that actually exhausts to the exterior. Older Jacksonville homes sometimes have inadequate bathroom ventilation that leads to chronic moisture problems. We address these underlying conditions as part of every bathroom renovation.

Wall removal and open-concept conversions are increasingly popular in Jacksonville's older housing stock, where 1970s and 1980s floor plans can feel compartmentalized by today's standards. Every wall removal requires assessment of whether the wall is load-bearing. Load-bearing walls require engineered beam systems, properly sized posts, and foundation support at the new post locations. We coordinate this engineering and obtain proper permits before any structural work begins.

Interior renovation in Jacksonville also involves making smart material choices for long-term durability. Luxury vinyl plank flooring has become a dominant choice in Jacksonville homes for its combination of durability, water resistance, and aesthetic appeal in Florida's climate. Porcelain tile is preferred for wet areas. We help clients navigate material selections based on their lifestyle, pets, children, and tolerance for maintenance.

Financing options through our third-party lending partners are available for interior renovation projects of all sizes. Ask about available loan products during your estimate consultation.

Frequently Asked Questions — Interior Renovations in Jacksonville

How much does an interior renovation cost in Jacksonville?

Interior renovation costs range widely based on scope. A bathroom renovation runs $15,000-$45,000; a kitchen renovation $25,000-$80,000; a full interior renovation of a 2,000 sq ft home can range from $80,000 to $200,000 or more depending on finish level.

Do interior renovations require permits in Jacksonville?

Cosmetic work — flooring, paint, replacing fixtures without moving plumbing — typically does not require permits. Structural changes, plumbing relocations, electrical panel work, and wall removal all require permits. We advise on requirements and pull all necessary permits.

How long does an interior renovation take?

A single bathroom renovation typically takes 4-8 weeks. A kitchen renovation takes 6-12 weeks. A full interior gut renovation takes 3-6 months. We establish a realistic schedule before work begins.

Can I remove a wall in my Jacksonville home to create an open floor plan?

Yes, but load-bearing walls require engineering. We assess every wall before removal, coordinate structural engineers when needed, and build proper beam and post systems to safely support the structure.

What flooring works best in Jacksonville's climate?

Luxury vinyl plank (LVP) is an excellent choice for Jacksonville because it resists moisture, tolerates temperature fluctuations, and holds up under heavy use. Porcelain tile is preferred in wet areas. We advise on flooring selection based on each room's use and your household's lifestyle.
