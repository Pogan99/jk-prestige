Steel Building Construction Services — Florida

End-to-end delivery for pre-engineered and conventional steel frame commercial facilities.

Pre-Engineered Metal Buildings

Factory-fabricated rigid frame systems from AISC-certified manufacturers (Nucor, BlueScope, Robertson, MBCI). Eave heights from 14 to 50+ feet, clear spans to 300+ feet, single-slope and gable-roof configurations. JK Prestige manages manufacturer selection, RFI process during detailing, anchor bolt inspection, and erection sequence coordination.

Conventional Structural Steel

AISC 360-compliant structural steel frame for multi-story commercial, mixed-use, and industrial facilities where irregular geometry, architectural integration, or seismic detailing requirements exceed the capability of standard pre-engineered systems. Coordinate with structural engineer of record, steel fabricator, and erector under a unified schedule.

Florida Wind Load Compliance

Florida's 150–170 mph basic wind speed zone requires rigorous attention to component and cladding (C&C) pressures on metal panel systems, girt and purlin deflection limits, and connection design at eave struts and base plates. JK Prestige coordinates shop drawing review against the ASCE 7 C&C pressure maps specific to the project's Risk Category and exposure classification.

Foundation & Anchor Bolt Systems

Pre-engineered building foundation design is driven by the manufacturer's anchor bolt reactions — uplift, shear, and overturning at each frame location. JK Prestige coordinates the geotechnical engineer's foundation recommendation, the structural engineer of record's foundation design, and the anchor bolt template installation to ensure bolt pattern, projection, and location tolerance meet the manufacturer's erection manual requirements before concrete placement.

Roofing, Insulation & Wall Panel

Standing seam metal roofing (Kynar 500 or equivalent fluoropolymer finish for Florida coastal durability), insulation systems (single-layer, double-layer, or spray polyurethane foam for energy code compliance), and metal wall panels (R-panel, insulated sandwich panel) with appropriate finish systems for exposure to Florida's coastal environment.

Our Steel Building Construction Process — 5 Steps

Procurement-driven scheduling that prevents fabrication lead times from controlling the project.

Manufacturer Selection & Early Procurement

JK Prestige initiates steel manufacturer selection and purchase order at or before permit submission. Pre-engineered building fabrication runs 10–16 weeks, and this lead time controls the project schedule if procurement is delayed. Early purchase locks in steel pricing in a volatile market and ensures the manufacturer's engineering is complete before erection crew mobilization is needed.

Foundation & Site Work

Foundation design is coordinated between the structural engineer of record (who seals the foundation drawings in Florida) and the manufacturer's anchor bolt reactions. Anchor bolt templates are installed, surveyed, and verified against the manufacturer's approved anchor bolt plan before foundation concrete placement. A failed anchor bolt survey costs days; a survey after the fact against hardened concrete costs tens of thousands.

Steel Delivery & Erection

JK Prestige coordinates steel delivery sequencing with the erection crew's lift plan, crane capacity, and site access routing. Primary frame erection is followed by secondary framing (purlins, girts), roof deck, and wall skin installation in a sequence that minimizes weather exposure to the unprotected structure and maintains safe working loads on the erection crane.

Roofing, Insulation & Skin

Standing seam metal roofing with manufacturer-required underlayment and insulation system is installed by JK Prestige's GAF Master Elite roofing crews, ensuring warranty coverage on the roof system. Wall panel installation, trim, doors, windows, and sealant systems complete the building envelope before interior MEP rough-in begins.

MEP, Finishes & CO

Interior MEP rough-in, office framing and drywall (self-performed by JK Prestige), LED lighting, concrete floor (if slab is poured after structure to protect against erection equipment damage), site paving, and final inspections are sequenced on the master schedule for on-time CO delivery. Steel building projects close out with a manufacturer's erection inspection report confirming ASCE 7 compliance.

Steel Building FAQ — Florida

Common questions from commercial developers, industrial operators, and building owners.

What is a pre-engineered steel building and how is it different from conventional steel construction?

A pre-engineered steel building uses factory-fabricated primary and secondary framing designed as an integrated system by the building manufacturer. Conventional steel uses AISC-specified members cut and fabricated per project-specific structural engineer drawings. Pre-engineered systems offer faster erection and lower cost for standard clear-span applications; conventional steel offers more design flexibility for irregular geometries or complex architectural integration.

What Florida building code requirements apply to steel buildings?

Florida Building Code incorporates AISC 360, ASCE 7-22 for wind and seismic loads, and Florida-specific high-wind provisions. Jacksonville is in a 150+ mph basic wind speed zone requiring careful attention to component and cladding pressures on metal panels and girts. JK Prestige coordinates structural engineering peer review and shop drawing approval on all steel projects.

How long does it take to erect a steel building in Florida?

A pre-engineered steel building takes 14–22 weeks from order to erection completion: 10–16 weeks manufacturer fabrication plus 2–6 weeks on-site erection. Conventional structural steel runs 12–20 weeks fabrication plus varying erection time. JK Prestige initiates steel procurement at or before permit submission to prevent fabrication lead times from controlling the project schedule.

What does a commercial steel building cost per square foot in Florida?

A basic pre-engineered steel building shell typically has a total installed cost of $60 to $110 per square foot depending on clear height, bay spacing, and finish level. Conventional structural steel frame for multi-story commercial buildings runs $35 to $70 per square foot for the structural frame alone. JK Prestige provides complete project estimates, not component-only pricing, within 48 hours.
